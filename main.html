COUNCIL OF LEADERS

The Council of Leaders is a dynamic student-led organization within the Senior High School (SHS) Department of PHINMA – University of Iloilo. For the past four years, it has been at the forefront of fostering leadership, collaboration, and student engagement. As the primary governing body representing senior high school students, it ensures their voices are heard and organizes impactful school-wide events and initiatives.

Founded by visionary educators Sir Czar Oneill Pamplona and Ma’am Lynie Aguirre, the Council of Leaders was created to provide a platform for student leaders to develop their skills, take on responsibilities, and contribute to a vibrant and inclusive school environment. Their collaboration led to the establishment of structured leadership programs, student-led projects, and various extracurricular activities that continue to shape the SHS community today.

Since its inception, the Council of Leaders has played a crucial role in planning and executing major school events, including academic competitions, leadership training sessions, outreach programs, and student engagement activities. It also serves as a bridge between students and faculty, addressing student concerns and promoting a culture of responsibility, teamwork, and excellence.

Over the years, the council has evolved, with each batch of student leaders bringing fresh ideas while upholding the core values established by its founders. Today, the Council of Leaders of the SHS Department of PHINMA – University of Iloilo remains a vital institution, continuously empowering students to develop their leadership potential and actively participate in shaping their school community.

SMHAP

Established on August 7, 2023, SMHAP is the official student organization for the Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMS) strand at PHINMA University of Iloilo SHS Department. We are thrilled to welcome our Technical-Vocational Livelihood (TVL) and General Academic Strand (GAS) students to this vibrant community. As you embark on this exciting journey, SMHAP is here to support you in this new chapter of your Senior High School life.

SCIMATH

SciMath was established on August 9, 2023, with the mission of fueling curiosity, sparking conversation, and pushing the boundaries of scientific and mathematical knowledge. As the official Science and Mathematics organization of the PHINMA University of Iloilo Senior High School Department, SciMath serves as a hub for students passionate about exploring the wonders of science and the logic of mathematics.

The organization provides a platform for intellectually curious students to engage in hands-on experiments, mathematical problem-solving, research initiatives, and academic competitions. Through workshops, seminars, and interactive activities, SciMath aims to cultivate analytical thinking, innovation, and a deep appreciation for the role of science and mathematics in everyday life.

Beyond academics, SciMath fosters collaboration and critical thinking, encouraging members to question, explore, and seek solutions to real-world challenges. Whether you're drawn to scientific discoveries, mathematical theories, or technological advancements, SciMath provides an environment where students can expand their knowledge, challenge their perspectives, and develop skills essential for future careers in STEM fields.

Join us in uncovering the mysteries of the universe, solving complex equations, and celebrating the beauty of logic and discovery. With SciMath, the pursuit of knowledge never ends it only grows stronger.

BUSINESS CATALYST

The Business Expo 2023, titled "The Starters," was officially launched on August 7, 2023, captivating the entire PHINMA University of Iloilo community with its extravagant display and diverse array of innovative products. This highly anticipated event served as a platform for student exhibitors from various academic strands to showcase their entrepreneurial skills, creativity, and business acumen.

The expo highlighted a wide selection of distinctive and inventive products, ranging from handcrafted goods and fashion items to cutting-edge technology and sustainable solutions. Each booth reflected the dedication and ingenuity of the participating students, demonstrating their ability to conceptualize, develop, and present marketable products.

Beyond the product showcases, "The Starters" also featured engaging activities such as live product demonstrations, business pitching sessions, and interactive discussions with industry experts and mentors. These activities provided students with valuable insights into entrepreneurship, marketing strategies, and financial management, equipping them with real-world skills essential for future business ventures.

Faculty members, school administrators, and guests expressed their admiration for the students' entrepreneurial spirit, acknowledging the event as a testament to PHINMA University of Iloilo's commitment to fostering innovation, leadership, and business excellence.

Through "The Starters," the Business Expo 2023 successfully bridged the gap between education and enterprise, allowing students to experience the realities of business while inspiring them to pursue their entrepreneurial aspirations. The event not only celebrated student talent but also reinforced the importance of creativity, collaboration, and resilience in the ever-evolving world of business.

SCAENA EXCELSIOR

Scaena Excelsior was established on August 9, 2023, as the official drama club of the PHINMA University of Iloilo Basic Education Department – Senior High School. It serves as a vibrant and inclusive community where creativity takes center stage, providing students with a platform to express themselves through the performing arts.

The club welcomes budding actors, directors, playwrights, and stage enthusiasts alike, fostering a supportive environment where members can develop their theatrical skills, confidence, and artistic vision. Through stage performances, scriptwriting workshops, and immersive acting exercises, Scaena Excelsior nurtures talent and encourages self-expression.

Beyond performances, the drama club promotes teamwork, discipline, and storytelling, allowing students to explore different perspectives and emotions through acting. Whether you're stepping onto the stage for the first time or honing your craft, Scaena Excelsior provides opportunities to grow and shine in the world of theater.

Join us in bringing stories to life, embracing creativity, and making every performance an unforgettable experience. The stage is yours step into the spotlight with Scaena Excelsior!

SINDAYAW

On August 19, 2023, the Official Dance Troupe of PHINMA University of Iloilo SHS Department—Sindayaw—was officially established. This dynamic dance ensemble serves as a platform for passionate and talented students to embrace their love for dance, refine their skills, and showcase their talents on stage.

Sindayaw provides a space where students can unlock their full potential, turning their passion for dance into captivating performances. Whether through contemporary, hip-hop, cultural, or modern choreography, members of the troupe are encouraged to express themselves artistically while developing discipline, teamwork, and confidence.

By joining this prestigious dance ensemble, students become part of a community that nurtures talent, fosters dedication, and creates a legacy of outstanding performances. Every movement and performance is a testament to the hard work, creativity, and perseverance of its members.

As the official dance company of PHINMA University of Iloilo SHS Department, Sindayaw continues to uplift the school’s cultural and artistic spirit, delivering electrifying performances that leave a lasting impact.

To all aspiring dancers—this is your time to shine. Let go of hesitation and step into the spotlight. Join Sindayaw to embark on a journey of passion, artistry, and excellence.

SINAG GLEE

Sinag was established on August 19, 2023, with the belief that music has the power to bring people together and create lasting memories. As the official music ensemble of the PHINMA University of Iloilo Senior High School Department, Sinag serves as a platform for students to explore their musical talents, express their creativity, and develop a deep appreciation for music.

Our dedicated team of experienced instructors and passionate members is committed to guiding students through a wide range of musical genres, from classical to contemporary. Whether you are an experienced performer or just beginning your musical journey, Sinag provides an inclusive and nurturing environment where you can refine your skills, gain confidence, and grow as a musician.

Through performances, workshops, and collaborations, Sinag continues to inspire and unite the school community through the power of music. As we work together to create beautiful harmonies and unforgettable performances, we invite all aspiring musicians to join us in celebrating the art of music.
